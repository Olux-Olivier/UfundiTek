About Us

Who are we ?

UfundiTek is a congolese company with a majority congolese capital. We are active in the Congolese market and work in partnership with a number congolese companies and internationally renowned firms.

We offer various services, including :

  • Civil engineering
  • Steel erection
  • Energy.
  • Solar power system
  • Electrical systems
  • Maintenance.

Our Services

Civil engineering

UfundiTek support you at every stage of your project :

  • Project development
  • Design phase
  • Layout phase
  • Procurement phase
  • Construction phase
  • Post-Construction phase.

Steel erection

UfundiTek and its international partners offer you an economical, lightweight and fast solution for erecting steel buildings :

  • Industrial buildings : production sites, showrooms, workshops
  • Warehouse : general storage building, agricultural building

Energy

UfundiTek offers you solutions for heating and air conditioning systems :

  • Industrial cooling
  • Domestic cooling
  • Air conditioning
  • Heating systems.

Solar power systems

UfundiTek offers solutions to support the energy transition :

  • Sizing of photovoltaic power plants
  • Installation of photovoltaic systems
  • Photovoltaic system maintenance
  • Energy saving support

Electrical system

UfundiTek supports you in electrification projects, including :

  • Conception and design of electrical installations
  • Installation of electrical panels and cabinets
  • Inspection of installations
  • Lighting systems

Maintenance

UfundiTek supports its customers in the maintenance of mechanical and electrical equipment by offerin :

  • Large surface cleaning
  • Industrial painting
  • Machine cleaning and lubrication
  • Predictive, corrective and 4.0 maintenance of the equipement
